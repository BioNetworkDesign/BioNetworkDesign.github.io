“Life is movement”, the delicate and dynamic macroscopic phenomenons encoded by random physicochemical interactions between molecules.

Life has evolved various amazing regulatory mechanisms to control life activities and adapt to environmental changes. However, we still lack the ability to understand and control these complex mechanisms. I want to develop technologies to characterize the physicochemical laws behind the dynamic processes of life, and use these laws to simplify existing or design new regulatory elements for the applications of synthetic and systems biology. Currently, I am participating in the project of Professor Lou’s group, hoping to develop high-throughput measuring technology and powerful algorithms to generate mammalian cell regulatory elements with user-defined constraints, and apply them to various application fields such as vaccine design and gene therapy.

生命在于运动，一种基于分子间随机的物理化学作用决定的精巧动态的整体运动
尽管生命进化出了种类令人惊叹的调控形式来控制生命活动并适应环境变化，我们仍然缺乏对复杂调控形式的改造和控制能力。因此，我致力于发展技术来捕捉和预测生命中的动态过程的物理化学规律，并用于简化已有的或设计新的调控元件。目前，我正在参与娄教授组的课题，希望发展高通量观测技术以及算法来生成和预测具有多样性和高效性能的哺乳动物细胞调控元件，并应用于疫苗设计和基因治疗。
